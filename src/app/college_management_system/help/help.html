Question: How do I register as a new student?

Answer: Go to the Student Registration Form and fill in your Name, Email, DOB, Department, and other required details. Then click 'Register'.

Question: How can I enroll in a course?

Answer: Once registered, the admin will assign you to courses in the Enrollment Panel. You can then view your courses from your dashboard.

Question: I forgot my password. What should I do?

Answer: Click on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

Question: How can I see my marks?

Answer: Login as a student and go to the 'My Marks' section where your marks for each course will be displayed.
